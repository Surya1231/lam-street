About Lam Street

Lamp Street uses a unique technology in which special resins impart extra strength to its laminates, making them highly resistant to scratch and abrasion. Lamp Street' exotic range of decorative laminates is characterized by higher color fastness and the best bonding properties with substrates available in the market today.
